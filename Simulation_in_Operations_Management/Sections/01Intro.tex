\section{Introduction and Objective}
\label{sec:intro}

% ~0.5 page. State the business problem and the simulation objective.
FermaCore Labs operates a tablet production line that combines batch-type and
rate-based operations. Management's \emph{2030 plan} aims to maximise output
within the \emph{existing} capacity before committing to a capital expansion.
This study uses discrete-event simulation (DES) in AnyLogic to (i) assess the
current operational performance, (ii) locate the bottleneck, (iii) quantify the
effect of targeted improvements, and (iv) determine the maximum demand the line
can absorb on the path to 2030.

The study follows the General Simulation Methodology \autocite{rossetti2016}.

\paragraph{Scope.} We address all mandatory requirements and the three specific
issues (bottleneck identification, improvement design, performance towards 2030),
and add one Industry~4.0 scenario (sensor-based reduction of tablet-press scrap
from \SI{10}{\percent} to \SI{4}{\percent}).
